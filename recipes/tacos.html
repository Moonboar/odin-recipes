Tacos

Description

Delicious street food from Mexico.

Ingredients

  • Corn tortillas
  • Cilantro
  • Onion
  • Meat of choice
  • Limes

Steps

  1. Cook the meat in a pan over medium heat
  2. Chop the cilantro and onions into small pieces
  3. Cut the limes into wedges
  4. Warm the tortillas in a pan over low heat
  5. Place meat, cilantro, and onions inside the tortilla
  6. Garnish with lime juice
