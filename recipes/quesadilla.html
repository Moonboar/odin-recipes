Quesadilla

Description

A yummy Mexican dish made with tortillas and cheese. You can add other ingredients to spice it up!

Ingredients

  • Flour tortillas
  • Pepperjack cheese
  • Black beans
  • Jalapenos

Steps

  1. Heat a pan over medium heat, adding butter to the pan
  2. Build the quesadilla with the ingredients between two tortillas
  3. Place quesadilla in pan, cooking until the cheese is melted
